Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Foundly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Foundly mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Name, email address, and password (or Google account details if signing in with Google)
  • Profile information: role, skills, interests, bio, location, experience level, and profile photo
  • Startup ideas you post
  • Messages you send to matches
  • Swipe activity (who you connect with or skip)

We also collect automatically:

  • Device push notification token (to send match and message alerts)
  • App usage data (e.g. login timestamps)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match you with other founders based on compatibility
  • To enable real-time messaging between matched users
  • To send push notifications for matches, messages, and idea interests
  • To display your profile to other users in the Discover feed (unless you disable discoverability)
  • To improve and maintain the app

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider (data stored in secure cloud infrastructure)
  • Google Firebase — for delivering push notifications to Android devices
  • Render — our backend hosting provider
  • Other users — your profile (name, photo, role, skills, bio, location) is visible to other Foundly users in the Discover feed

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security policies. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We use HTTPS for all data in transit.

5. Your Rights & Controls

  • Discoverability — you can hide your profile from the Discover feed in Settings at any time
  • Edit or delete — you can edit your profile or delete your account at any time from the Settings screen
  • Data deletion — deleting your account permanently removes your profile, matches, messages, and ideas from our systems
  • Block users — you can block any user from the chat screen

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted within 24 hours.

7. Children's Privacy

Foundly is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
